Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find scholarships that match my profile?

Use our advanced search and filtering system on the Scholarships page. You can filter by class level, income group, state, and field of study to find the most relevant opportunities.

Are all the scholarships on your platform verified?

Yes, we verify all scholarship information with official sources before listing them. We regularly update deadlines and requirements to ensure accuracy.

How can I apply for scholarships through your platform?

We provide information and guidance about scholarships, but applications are submitted directly to the scholarship providers. We offer sample forms and application tips to help you.

Do you provide career counseling services?

Yes, we offer career counseling and can connect you with mentors and career counselors. Contact us directly to learn more about our mentorship programs.

How can my organization partner with Educate India?

We welcome partnerships with foundations, NGOs, universities, and government bodies. Please email us at partnerships@eduaccessindia.org with your proposal.

Is your platform free to use?

Yes, all our information and tools are completely free to use. We believe in democratizing access to education information.
