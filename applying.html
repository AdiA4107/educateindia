How to Apply for an Education Loan

Eligibility & Documents

  • Indian citizen, confirmed admission (offer/ I-20/ CAS/ admission letter)
  • Co-borrower with verifiable income; collateral if required by lender
  • Documents: KYC, admission letter, fee structure, mark sheets, income proofs, bank statements, property papers (if collateral)

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Estimate need with EMI Calculator and select lender category (PSB/Private/NBFC)
  2. Check eligibility and interest reset terms; shortlist 2–3 lenders
  3. Prepare documents; apply online and schedule verification
  4. Underwriting & sanction letter; complete collateral formalities (if any)
  5. Disbursement as per institution fee schedule

Tips

  • Prefer repo-linked floating rates at PSBs if time permits
  • Compare APR including fees and insurance, not headline rate alone
  • Build a strong co-borrower profile; maintain clean bank statements

How to Apply for Scholarships

Where to Find Scholarships

  • National Scholarship Portal (NSP) and state portals
  • University financial aid pages and departments
  • Private foundations, corporate CSR portals and verified listings here

Documents & Preparation

  • KYC, academic transcripts, income certificates, caste/category where applicable
  • Statement of Purpose, Letters of Recommendation, resume/CV
  • Proof of admission or enrollment (if required)

Application Checklist

  • Read eligibility carefully; prioritize fit over volume of applications
  • Customize SOP to scholarship objectives; quantify impact
  • Submit before deadline; keep copies and track status
