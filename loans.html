PM Vidyalakshmi Portal

Understanding Different Loan Types

Public Sector Banks (PSBs)

Government-backed loans with subsidies and guarantees

₹7.5L Collateral-Free Limit
7.5% - 11.15% Interest Rate Range
15-30 days Processing Time

Advantages

  • Collateral-free loans up to ₹7.5L under government guarantee
  • Lowest interest rates in the market
  • Government subsidies available (CSIS & CGFSEL)
  • No academic score requirements
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Applying

Limitations

  • Longer processing time (15-30 days)
  • Manual documentation and verification required
  • Limited digital experience
  • Higher NPA rates due to riskier borrowers
  • Applying

Fast, digital-first lending with specialized education focus

Advantages

  • Fastest processing time (3-7 days)
  • Completely online application process
  • Lowest NPA rates in the industry
  • Specialized education loan expertise
  • Flexible repayment during moratorium
  • Applying

Limitations

  • Higher interest rates than PSBs
  • Strict academic requirements and scores
  • Higher co-applicant income requirements
  • Limited government subsidies
